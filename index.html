Privacy Policy — BizPOS

Last updated: June 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how BizPOS ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our Android, iOS, or Web application for point-of-sale billing and inventory management.

By creating an account or using BizPOS, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Who This Policy Applies To

BizPOS is a business tool used by shop owners, retailers, and their staff ("Admin" and "Cashier" accounts). Each Admin account represents a separate, isolated business. Data entered under one business account is never visible to another business.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account information

  • Email address and password (used for sign-in via Firebase Authentication)
  • A unique account/user ID generated automatically
  • Business profile details you choose to enter (shop name, address, contact info, logo)

2.2 Business and operational data

To provide the App's core functionality, we store the data you enter while using BizPOS, including:

  • Products, categories, brands, and inventory/stock records
  • Sales transactions and receipts
  • Customer records and customer ledgers
  • Supplier records, supplier ledgers, and purchase orders
  • Staff/cashier accounts, salary records, and expense entries
  • Reports generated from the above data

This data is stored both locally on your device (for offline use) and in our cloud backend (Firebase/Firestore), and is synced automatically when an internet connection is available.

2.3 Device and usage information

  • App activity such as feature usage, in order to operate and improve the App
  • Diagnostic and crash data, if Firebase Crashlytics/Analytics is enabled
  • Advertising identifiers, only if and when ads are shown (see Section 5)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account, including Admin and Cashier roles
  • To provide core App functionality: billing, inventory tracking, reporting, and offline sync
  • To keep your data isolated and secure per business
  • To detect, prevent, and fix technical issues
  • To display advertisements, where applicable, and measure their performance

We do not sell your personal or business data to third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Cloud data is stored using Google Firebase (Firestore, Authentication, and Storage), which encrypts data in transit (HTTPS/TLS).
  • Local data is stored in an on-device SQLite database for offline access and is synced to the cloud once a connection is restored.
  • Each business's data is logically isolated and is not accessible to other businesses or users.
  • Cashier accounts created by an Admin only have access to the functionality the Admin grants them.

5. Advertising

BizPOS may display advertisements through Google AdMob (on Android/iOS) and Google AdSense (on Web). Some advertising features may be enabled or disabled remotely and may not always be active.

When ads are shown, Google and its advertising partners may collect and use the following to serve and measure ads:

Data type Purpose
Advertising ID / Device identifiers Ad personalization and measurement
Approximate location (if permitted) Ad relevance
App interaction data Ad performance measurement

You can learn more about how Google uses this data at Google's Partner Sites policy, and manage ad personalization through your device's ad settings.

6. Data Sharing

We share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Google Firebase, our backend infrastructure provider, to store and process app data
  • With Google AdMob/AdSense, only when advertisements are displayed (see Section 5)
  • If required by law, regulation, legal process, or government request
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of BizPOS, our users, or the public

We do not share your business or financial data with any third party for marketing purposes.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your account and business data for as long as your account is active, or as needed to provide the App's services.

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at the email address below. Upon a verified request, we will delete your account data from our systems within a reasonable timeframe, except where retention is required by law.

8. Children's Privacy

BizPOS is a business management tool intended for shop owners, staff, and adult business users. It is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the relevant minimum age in your jurisdiction).

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
