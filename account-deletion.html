Delete Your BizPOS Account

Last updated: June 30, 2026

BizPOS gives you full control over your account and data. This page explains how to request deletion of your BizPOS account and the data associated with it.

How to Request Account Deletion

Step 1. Send an email to baloxtech@gmail.com from the email address registered with your BizPOS account.

Step 2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request – BizPOS"

Step 3. Include your registered email address and, if known, your business name so we can locate your account.

Step 4. We will verify your request and confirm via email once your account and data have been deleted.

Email us to request deletion
⏱ Processing time: Account deletion requests are processed within 30 days of verification.

What Gets Deleted

Data type Deleted?
Account credentials (email, password) Deleted
Business profile (shop name, address, logo) Deleted
Products, inventory, and stock records Deleted
Sales transactions and receipts Deleted
Customer and supplier records/ledgers Deleted
Cashier/staff accounts linked to your business Deleted
Expense and report data Deleted

What May Be Retained

Some limited information may be retained for a short additional period where required by law or for legitimate business purposes, such as:

  • Financial transaction records required for tax, accounting, or legal compliance, retained for up to 12 months after deletion, after which they are permanently removed.
  • Anonymized or aggregated usage data that no longer identifies you or your business, which may be retained indefinitely for analytics purposes.
  • Records necessary to investigate fraud, abuse, or violations of our Terms of Service, retained only as long as legally necessary.

Multi-User (Cashier) Accounts

If your business uses Cashier accounts created by an Admin, deleting the Admin account will also delete all associated Cashier accounts and their access. Cashiers cannot request deletion of the Admin's business data themselves; only the Admin can initiate this request.
